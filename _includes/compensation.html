Compensation System

When a cheater is confirmed, FairPlay reduces their effective advantage and automatically compensates affected players — restoring fairness and trust on your server.

  • Automated victim detection and compensation events.
  • Configurable compensation rules (items, currency, XP, temporary buffs).
  • Anti-retaliation: penalize repeated offenders and revoke ill-gotten items.

The result: cheaters no longer profit from cheating, and legitimate players are restored — discouraging abuse.
