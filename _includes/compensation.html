Active Countermeasures

Why just kick a cheater when you can let the community fight back? When an unfair advantage is detected, FairPlay briefly empowers the victim with the exact same abilities to turn the tide.

  • Reach equalizer: If a cheater strikes with 5-block reach, their target instantly gets 5-block reach strictly against them.
  • Anti-retaliation logic: Only triggers against confirmed cheaters. Legitimate player combat remains 100% unaffected.
  • Active discouragement: Cheaters lose their core incentive when their victims become unkillable counter-threats.
